GlobeTrotter

The vision of GlobeTrotter is to transform travel planning into an engaging and seamless experience. The platform empowers users to dream, design, and organize trips effortlessly by combining flexibility, interactivity, and intelligent insights.

GlobeTrotter aims to make travel planning as exciting as the journey itself by allowing users to:

Explore global destinations

Visualize structured itineraries

Make cost-effective travel decisions

Collaborate and share plans within a community

The mission of the GlobeTrotter hackathon team is to build a user-centric and responsive travel planning application that simplifies complex, multi-city travel planning.

The platform focuses on providing intuitive tools that help users:

Organize trips efficiently

Stay within budget

Maintain full visibility of their travel journey

This is achieved through a well-designed relational database, scalable backend services, and a smooth, dynamic frontend experience.
